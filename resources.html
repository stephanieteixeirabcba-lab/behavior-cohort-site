A note on jurisdiction

Credentialing and scope-of-practice requirements vary internationally. The IBAO provides credentials recognized across many countries. If you hold a different credential (BCBA, QBA, or a regionally recognized equivalent), follow the supervision and scope standards from your credentialing body. When in doubt, consult your supervising BA.

Ground rules

No identifying client information, ever. De-identify thoroughly. Do not share details of a specific behavior plan — that is a conversation for your supervising BA. Treat everything shared in this space as confidential within the course.
