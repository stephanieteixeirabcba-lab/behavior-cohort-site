Your progress

Each participant has a private Google Sheet where homework submissions, attendance, and feedback are tracked. The link lives in your welcome email — you can also request it here if you've lost it.

How progress tracking works

This cohort doesn't use letter grades. Your progress sheet tracks:

  • Attendance — which meetings you've joined.
  • Submissions — which homework assignments you've turned in.
  • Feedback — written notes from me on each submission.

The sheet is shared only with you. I can see the full cohort in a master view; you only see your own.

Accessing your sheet

Check your welcome email for the link — it's unique to you. If you've lost it, email me and I'll resend.

Request new link

Why a Google Sheet and not the website?

A static website can't securely store private data. A per-participant Google Sheet is simple, reliable, and properly permissioned — each of you only sees your own.
